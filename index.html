The Respawn Banter

The Respawn Banter is a unique adventure and visual novel hybrid where you are bound to die during your exploration. In the afterlife, the goddess of life’s role extends beyond mere revival: she seizes every opportunity to humor the hero’s demise, making each encounter a one-of-a-kind witty exchange.

  • Wishlist now!

The Story

The game over screen has always been a mystery: what hides behind it?
It might surprise you that behind it lies the goddess of life, who, by making just an exception, brings the hero back to life to enable him to resume his arduous path to victory.
If only they would get along...

The Gameplay

Explore and avoid unfairly funny traps. Be ready to die, but always with a smile on your face!
But dying is not the end of the game, it's the start of the joke!
Experience countless dynamic discussions based on how you reach a game over!

Meet the characters

Get to know the cast of characters you will meet over the course of your multiple lives!
A stupid hero who cannot shut up for a single second? An extremely moody life goddess? What could possibly go wrong?
